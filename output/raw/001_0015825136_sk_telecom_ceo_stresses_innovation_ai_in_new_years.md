# SK Telecom CEO stresses innovation, AI in New Year&#x27;s message

**연합뉴스** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

SK Telecom-CEO messageSK Telecom CEO stresses innovation, AI in New Year&#x27;s message SEOUL, Jan. 2 (Yonhap) -- The head of SK Telecom Co. on Friday emphasized the need to embrace change and innovation as the company accelerates its push into the artificial intelligence (AI) business. &quot;All great changes initially seem impossible and demand patience, but they are ultimately remembered as brilliant growth,&quot; Chief Executive Officer (CEO) Jung Jai-hun said in a New Year&#x27;s message posted on the company&#x27;s newsroom. &quot;Our changes can be completed when we become one Dream Team. We need to set aside fear of change and become each other&#x27;s steadfast support.&quot; Jung said the company needs to strengthen its core telecommunications business, create new innovations and accelerate its AI transformation. &quot;We will create our own innovations to write a new history in AI,&quot; he said, adding that the AI transformation is necessary to improve daily life. SK Telecom, the country&#x27;s largest mobile carrier, has been seeking to expand its business portfolio into AI data center solutions.
