# HONDURAS FIRE

---

Dozens of businesses destroyed by a massive fire in Honduras&#x27; second largest cityVictims collect their belongings after an arson attack at a fireworks stand in the Medina Concepcion market in San Pedro Sula, Honduras, 29 December 2025. Dozens of businesses in the bustling market in San Pedro Sula, Honduras&#x27; second largest city, were destroyed by a massive blaze that started in a stall storing rockets and firecrackers, according to preliminary reports from the fire department.
