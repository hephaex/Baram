# Goodyear Tire Art Mascots 2025

**AP연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Goodyear Tire Art Mascots 2025IMAGE DISTRIBUTED FOR GOODYEAR - To celebrate the drive and outstanding performances this season by Ohio State and Miami, who will be competing in the 2025 College Football Playoff Quarterfinal at the 90th Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic, Goodyear commissioned larger-than-life tire sculptures of each team&#x27;s mascots. They were photographed with the Field Scovell Trophy on Sunday, Dec. 28, in Arlington, Texas. The Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic will kick off Wednesday, Dec. 31.
