# [News Today] 1st anniversary of Jeju Air tragedy

**KBS** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]One year has passed since the Jeju Air passenger plane crash that killed 179 people.At Muan International Airport, memorial events were held as bereaved families and citizens gathered to honor the victims.[REPORT]The end of the runway where 179 people lost their lives.A procession approaches the site after walking down the &quot;path of truth&quot; in memory of the victims.Bereaved families have spent the past year in pain as they called for the truth.People from across the nation join them in mourning for the victims.Son Ju-taek / Victim&#x27;s FamilyI earnestly ask you to help uncover the truth and punish those responsible.At the localizer mound blamed for exacerbating the disaster,a memorial Mass was held with representatives of bereaved families in attendance.They prayed for the souls to gain peace, and for the survivors to be healed.Ok Hyun-jin / Archbishop of GwangjuOur last farewell said here shows our love for one another, eases the pain, and lifts up hope.A joint food offering was arranged in front of the temporary memorial altar set up on the first floor of the airport terminal.Families of the 179 victims wept as they prayed for the souls of their loved ones.Kim Yu-jin / Head of group representing bereaved familiesA year has passed since the tragedy, but our hearts still remain on that day.The families are urging authorities to find out the truth so that such a tragedy will not be repeated.The Ministry of Land, Infrastructure and Transport and the bereaved families council held an official memorial ceremony at 10 a.m. on Monday at Muan International Airport.재난 희생자들의 명복을 빕니다. 확인되지 않은 정보 유포나 피해자에 대한 비난을 삼가주세요. 재난을 겪은 뒤 심리적인 어려움이 있는 경우 ☎02-2204-0001(국가트라우마센터) 또는 1577-0199(정신건강위기 상담전화)로 연락하시면 전문가의 상담을 받을 수 있습니다.※ 이 기사는 재난보도준칙을 준수하였습니다.■ 제보하기▷ 전화 : 02-781-1234, 4444▷ 이메일 : kbs1234@kbs.co.kr▷ 카카오톡 : &#x27;KBS제보&#x27; 검색, 채널 추가▷ 네이버, 유튜브에서 KBS뉴스를 구독해주세요!
