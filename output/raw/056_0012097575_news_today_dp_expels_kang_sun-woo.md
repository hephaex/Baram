# [News Today] DP expels Kang Sun-woo

**KBS** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]The Democratic Party has expelled lawmaker Kang Sun-woo over allegations of illicit so-called nomination donations.The Democratic Party has also moved to launch disciplinary proceedings against Kim Byung-kee, its former floor leader.He faces accusations of turning a blind eye to the alleged nomination funds and other irregularities.[REPORT]The Democratic Party convened an emergency Supreme Council meeting in the evening of New Year&#x27;s Day.During the session, it approved the expulsion of lawmaker Kang Sun-woo, mired in allegations of receiving 100 million won in connection with an election nomination.Park Soo-hyun / Senior spokesperson, Democratic PartyIn the event Kang wants to return to the party later, as a record still remains, this procedure enables a clear expulsion.Earlier on Thursday afternoon, three days after the allegations surfaced, Kang submitted her resignation and quit the party.Kang said she could no longer burden the party and vowed to actively cooperate with procedures requested by the party and the police investigation.Up to now, she has strongly denied the accusations by offering repeated explanations, but appears to have made the decision to quit amid an intensifying police probe and worsening public opinion.The Democratic Party also requested its ethics committee to decide on disciplinary measures against its former floor leader Kim Byung-kee, accused of turning a blind eye to money allegedly given for nominations and various other irregularities.Jung Chung-rae / Chair, Democratic PartyNo one can be an exception.We will cut off what must be cut off and move forward with what must continue.The People Power Party, which has called for a special counsel investigation into the alleged nomination money, criticized Kang&#x27;s departure as a face-saving maneuver.Choi Soo-jin / Senior floor spokesperson, People Power PartyWith illegality confirmed, attempting to end responsibility by quitting the party is an act of deceiving the public.Kang must immediately resign her seat in the National Assembly.From next week, police will begin a full investigation into the alleged nomination money, starting with questioning the complainants.They plan to first establish key facts, including when Kang became aware of the money, whether it was actually returned, and its possible link to the uncontested
