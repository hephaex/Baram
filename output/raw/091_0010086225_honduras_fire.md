# HONDURAS FIRE

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Dozens of businesses destroyed by a massive fire in Honduras&#x27; second largest cityMembers of the Honduran Fire Department are working alongside citizens to control the fire that broke out at a fireworks stand in the Medina Concepcion market in San Pedro Sula, Honduras, 29 December 2025. Dozens of businesses in the bustling market in San Pedro Sula, Honduras&#x27; second largest city, were destroyed by a massive blaze that started in a stall storing rockets and firecrackers, according to preliminary reports from the fire department.
