# (LEAD) Veteran actor Ahn Sung-ki in critical condition after hospitalization in state of cardiac arrest

**연합뉴스** | 2025-12-31 00:00 | 

---

(LEAD) actor-hospitalization(LEAD) Veteran actor Ahn Sung-ki in critical condition after hospitalization in state of cardiac arrest(ATTN: UPDATES with more details in last 6 paras) SEOUL, Dec. 31 (Yonhap) -- Veteran actor Ahn Sung-ki was rushed to a hospital in a state of cardiac arrest and is currently in critical condition, sources said Wednesday. Ahn was moved to the emergency room as of around 4 p.m. Tuesday after collapsing while choking on food at his home. The actor was admitted to an intensive care unit, according to the sources.

The 73-year-old has appeared in approximately 140 films during his career spanning over six decades. Ahn, often called the &quot;Nation&#x27;s Actor&quot; for his contributions to the industry, debuted in 1957 in &quot;The Twilight Train&quot; as a child actor. He went on to build one of the most prolific careers in Korean cinema, earning widespread recognition through roles in films including &quot;Silmido&quot; (2003), &quot;Hanbando&quot; (2006), &quot;Unbowed&quot; (2011) and &quot;Hansan: Rising Dragon&quot; (2022). He was named Best Actor numerous times at local film awards, including the Baeksang Arts Awards and the Blue Dragon Film Awards. Beyond his acting career, he has served as a representative for UNICEF since the early 1990s. During a 2022 interview, he revealed that he had been battling with blood cancer since 2019.
