# SPAIN FLOODS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Floods affect several towns in southern SpainMembers of the Andalusian regional forest firefighting unit Infoca, local policemen, and local workers remove a vehicle from a street during cleanup operations the morning after floods affected several parts of the town of Estacion de Cartama following the overflow of the Guadalhorce River, in Malaga, southern Spain, 28 December 2025. Heavy rains caused flooding in several towns in the province of Malaga last night, leading to evacuations, rescues, and property damage. EPA/CARLOS DIAZ
