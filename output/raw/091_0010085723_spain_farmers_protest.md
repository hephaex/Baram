# SPAIN FARMERS PROTEST

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

Farmers protest in SpainTractors drive on the C-25 motorway as it passes through Vilobi d&#x27;Onyar, Girona, Spain, 29 December 2025. Agricultural union Unio de Pagesos (UP) organized a protest with tractors to block the connection between Lleida and Girona, in protest against the &#x27;mismanagement of game animals&#x27; and to demand better prevention of livestock diseases such as contagious Lumpy Skin Disease (LSD).
