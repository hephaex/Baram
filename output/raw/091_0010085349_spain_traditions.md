# SPAIN TRADITIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

&#x27;The flour-covered ones&#x27; festival in SpainDisguised residents hold eggs and flour as they are prepared to take part in a flour battle called &#x27;Els Enfarinants&#x27; (The Flour-Covered Ones) in the town of Ibi, Alicante, eastern Spain, 28 December 2028. This battle is a tradition dating back to the 19th Century and held each 28 December, which is Spanish Fool&#x27;s Day. EPA/PABLO MIRANZO
