# [News Today] NK fires long-range cruise missiles

**KBS** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]North Korea says it test-fired long-range strategic cruise missiles, releasing photos of the launch.Leader Kim Jong-un, who oversaw the drill, claimed the test was an exercise of the country&#x27;s right to self-defense.[REPORT]North Korea’s Korean Central News Agency says that the regime test-fired long-range strategic cruise missiles into the West Sea on Sunday.It also revealed eight photos of the firing, including scenes of the missiles being launched and striking a building.The launching drill was overseen by North Korean leader Kim Jong-un.The state news agency reported that the missiles flew for about 10,000 seconds along a flight course preset in the sky over the West Sea and precisely hit the target.The KCNA said that after watching the drill, Kim expressed great satisfaction, assessing that it demonstrated the absolute reliability and combat readiness of the North&#x27;s strategic counterattack capability.He insisted that the launch was the exercise of his country’s right to self-defense.The North Korean leader said that, faced with various security threats, it is Pyongyang’s responsible exercise of the right to self-defense to check the reliability and rapid response of components of North Korea&#x27;s nuclear deterrence and to continuously demonstrate its power.He then pledged to devote all the regime’s efforts to the unlimited and sustainable development of the state nuclear combat force.South Korea’s Joint Chiefs of Staff said that it detected multiple missiles launched from the Sunan area near Pyongyang at around 8 a.m. on Sunday.A JCS official said that North Korea may conduct more missile tests ahead of the year-end and New Year’s season.■ 제보하기▷ 전화 : 02-781-1234, 4444▷ 이메일 : kbs1234@kbs.co.kr▷ 카카오톡 : &#x27;KBS제보&#x27; 검색, 채널 추가▷ 네이버, 유튜브에서 KBS뉴스를 구독해주세요!
