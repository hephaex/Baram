# PERU NEW YEAR TRADITIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-31 00:00 | 

---

Peruvians prepare to burn effigies of public figures on New Year&#x27;s EveNew Year&#x27;s Eve effigies depicting controversial Peruvian public figures are displayed at a market in Lima, Peru, 30 December 2025. The effigies, traditionally burned at year&#x27;s end to symbolize letting go of the past, include former president Dina Boluarte and popular TikToker Jusseira Mayumi, known as &#x27;Moca&#x27;.
