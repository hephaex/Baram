Malaysia&#x27;s 2025 exports hit 30 billion euros as of NovemberA reach stacker holding a cargo container is seen at Port Klang, outside Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, 29 December 2025. According to Malaysia External Trade Development Corp chairman Reezal Merican Naina Merican, Malaysia&#x27;s export value reached 1.45 trillion Malaysia Ringgit (MYR), about 30 billion euros, for the period of January to November 2025, while total trade hit 2.77 trillion MYR, about 58 billion euros, for the same period.
