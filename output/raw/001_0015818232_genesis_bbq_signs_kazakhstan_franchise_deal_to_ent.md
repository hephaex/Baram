# Genesis BBQ signs Kazakhstan franchise deal to enter Central Asian market

**연합뉴스** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

Genesis BBQ-Kazakhstan Genesis BBQ signs Kazakhstan franchise deal to enter Central Asian market SEOUL, Dec. 29 (Yonhap) -- Genesis BBQ Co., a South Korean fried chicken franchise operator, said Monday it has entered the Central Asian restaurant market after signing a master franchise agreement with a Kazakh company. The company recently signed the deal with Restopark KZ LLP, a Kazakhstan-based restaurant and entertainment firm, to open flagship outlets in Almaty and other major cities in the country, Genesis BBQ said in a press release. Under the agreement, the franchisee is authorized not only to open and operate outlets in the designated territory but also to grant sub-franchise rights to third parties. &quot;Kazakhstan is a strategic base for expansion across Central Asia. Building on operations in Kazakhstan, we will explore new business opportunities in Uzbekistan and Kyrgyzstan,&quot; a company official said.
