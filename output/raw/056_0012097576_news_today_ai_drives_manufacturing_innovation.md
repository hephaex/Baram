# [News Today] AI drives manufacturing innovation

**KBS** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]Artificial intelligence is reshaping Korea&#x27;s manufacturing sector.So far, 17% of domestic manufacturers have introduced AI into their operations.On factory floors, AI is driving efficiency, precision, and a fundamental change in how things are made.We take a look.[REPORT]This is a factory manufacturing auto parts.A robot lifts heavy parts and attaches them to an axis at a designated angle so that workers can assemble them.Artificial intelligence is activated with the start of the manufacturing process, accumulating data in real time.AI analyzes data collected from 2.4 million products annually and provides solutions on where and how to improve quality.Lee Do-yun / KBS reporterThese are finished parts. Each has a QR code containing manufacturing information that can be traced at any time if issues occur.Kim Min-kyu / Taelim Industrial Co., Ltd.The automatically stored data is used to identify what errors caused defective products and when they occurred.This factory manufacturing diamond wire saws for polishing semiconductor wafers is living proof of the benefits of AI.Defect inspection, which requires precise judgment to distinguish defects from light reflections, used to be performed only by highly skilled workers.Having learned from more than 80,000 images of defective products, the AI can now detect defects better than humans.Kim Sung-kyu / CEO, Shinhan DiamondAI can operate around the clock without human intervention.Our goal is 100% defect detection.Currently, only 17% of domestic manufacturers have introduced artificial intelligence.However, 40% of businesses are considering adopting it.For manufacturing companies, AI is no longer a choice but a necessity for survival.■ 제보하기▷ 전화 : 02-781-1234, 4444▷ 이메일 : kbs1234@kbs.co.kr▷ 카카오톡 : &#x27;KBS제보&#x27; 검색, 채널 추가▷ 네이버, 유튜브에서 KBS뉴스를 구독해주세요!
