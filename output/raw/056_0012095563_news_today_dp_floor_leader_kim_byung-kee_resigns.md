# [News Today] DP floor leader Kim Byung-kee resigns

**KBS** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]Democratic Party floor leader Kim Byung-kee has stepped down from his post.His resignation comes amid mounting allegations of preferential treatment involving himself and his family.He apologized, saying his actions fell short of public expectations.[REPORT]Democratic Party floor leader Kim Byung-kee has apologized over allegations against him and stepped down from his post.He announced his resignation at a party policy meeting on Tuesday morning, saying his actions fell short of public expectations.Kim Byung-kee / Floor leader, Democratic PartyAs long as I&#x27;m at the center of these allegations, I will become a burden to the Democratic Party and the Lee Jae Myung administration.Kim’s resignation comes just six months after he was elected as the first floor leader of the ruling party under the Lee Jae Myung administration.Kim had clashed with former aides over allegations of preferential treatment involving himself and his family.From last Wednesday, a series of claims were made, starting with allegations that Kim received an expensive hotel accommodation voucher from Korean Air.He had strongly denied the allegations and repeatedly said he had no intention to step down.However, allegations involving family members, including claims that his spouse used a district council&#x27;s corporate credit card, soon surfaced, and sentiment within the party began to turn against him.The situation escalated further on Monday, after reports alleged that Kim, while serving as secretary of the Seoul party’s nomination committee during the 2022 local elections, had failed to act despite being aware of allegations involving a 100 million won payment tied to DP lawmaker Kang Sun-woo&#x27;s camp.Police have received complaints related to the allegations involving Kim and his family and launched an investigation.Under party rules, a new floor leader must be elected within a month.■ 제보하기▷ 전화 : 02-781-1234, 4444▷ 이메일 : kbs1234@kbs.co.kr▷ 카카오톡 : &#x27;KBS제보&#x27;
