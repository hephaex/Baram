# THAILAND ELECTIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Prime minister candidates&#x27; registration for 2026 general election in ThailandIncumbent Thai Prime Minister and Bhumjaithai Party&#x27;s prime ministerial candidate Anutin Charnvirakul draws the number 37 for his party&#x27;s election campaign during the prime minister candidates registration for the 2026 general election in Bangkok, Thailand, 28 December 2025. Thailand begins nationwide registration of constituency candidates to kick off the general election scheduled for 08 February 2026 after Prime Minister Anutin Charnvirakul dissolved the House of Representatives.
