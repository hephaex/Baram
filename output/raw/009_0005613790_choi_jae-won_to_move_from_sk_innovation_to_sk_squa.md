# Choi Jae-won to move from SK Innovation to SK Square

**매일경제** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Choi Jae-won, senior vice chairman of SK innovation, is set to move to SK square to strengthen the group’s artificial intelligence and semiconductor business capabilities, industry sources said Monday.Choi is expected to assume the role of senior vice chairman at SK square effective Jan. 1 next year.SK square was launched in 2021 following a spinoff from SK telecom and serves as the SK Group’s investment arm, focusing on global investments in AI and semiconductors. The company oversees key subsidiaries including SK hynix, with the goal of enhancing corporate value.At SK square, Choi is expected to leverage his global network to support financing activities and help expand new investments centered on AI and semiconductor technologies, further strengthening the company’s investment capabilities.
