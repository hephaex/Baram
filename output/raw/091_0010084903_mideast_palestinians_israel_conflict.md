# MIDEAST PALESTINIANS ISRAEL CONFLICT

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Israeli military operation in the Palestinian village of QabatiyaIsraeli troops during a military operation in the Palestinian village of Qabatiya, near the West Bank city of Jenin, 27 December 2025. Israeli troops started a military operation after a Palestinian from Qabatiya vilage killed two Israelis in a stabbing and car-ramming attack in northern Israel on December 26. Israeli forces have continued their operation against Qabatiya for the second consecutive day, imposing a tight siege accompanied by mass filed interrogations of dozens of residents, forced displacement, widespread home raids and searches, and massive destruction of infrastructure.
