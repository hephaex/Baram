# Coupang founder apologizes for massive data leak

**연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Coupang founder-data leak Coupang founder apologizes for massive data leak By Kang Jae-eun SEOUL, Dec. 28 (Yonhap) -- The founder of e-commerce giant Coupang Inc., Kim Bom-suk, on Sunday apologized for a massive data breach that affected nearly two-thirds of the population in South Korea. &quot;As the founder and the chairman of the board, I sincerely apologize on behalf of all the employees at Coupang,&quot; Kim was quoted as saying in a written statement issued by the company. The apology came about a month since the personal information of about 34 million customers of Coupang was leaked.
