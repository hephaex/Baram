# USA MONKS PEACE WALK

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Buddhist monks continue 120-day, 2,300-mile pilgrimage to Washington, D.C. to promote peaceVenerable Monk Bhikkhu Pannakara greets well-wishers while leading a group of Buddhist monks during their 2,300-mile peace walk in Fayetteville, Georgia, USA, 29 December 2025. The monks, from the Huong Dao Vipassana Bhavana Center in Fort Worth, Texas, are on a planned 120-day &#x27;Walk for Peace&#x27; to Washington, DC, to promote peace, loving-kindness, compassion, mindful awareness, unity, and healing.
