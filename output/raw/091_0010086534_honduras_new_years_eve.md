# HONDURAS NEW YEARS EVE

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-31 00:00 | 

---

Effigies of Honduran politicians to be thrown into &#x27;bonfire&#x27; on New Year&#x27;s EveLuis Lagos arranges figures of (L-R) Honduran president-elect Nasry &#x27;Tito&#x27; Asfura, and presidential candidates Salvador Nasralla and Rixi Moncada at his upholstery workshop in Tegucigalpa, Honduras, 30 December 2025. The effigies made by artisans of personalities such as Asfura, Nasralla, and Moncada, among other politicians, will be burned as a symbolic and peaceful protest on New Year&#x27;s Eve 2025.
