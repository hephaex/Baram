# NIGERIA CAR CRASH ANTHONY JOSHUA

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

British boxer Anthony Joshua injured in Nigeria car crash, two killedThe broken window of an SUV carrying former world heavyweight champion Anthony Joshua at the scene of a car crash in Ogun, Nigeria, 29 December 2025. Joshua suffered minor injuries in the collision, which killed two other passengers, according to local police. The British boxer was traveling in the rear of the vehicle on the Lagos-Ibadan Expressway near Sagamu, his ancestral hometown, when the accident occurred. EPA/EMMANUEL ADEGBOYE
