# THAILAND ELECTIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Prime minister candidates&#x27; registration for 2026 general election in ThailandPheu Thai Party&#x27;s prime ministerial candidate Yotsanun Wongsawat (C), alongside the second candidate for prime minister Julapun Amornvivat (R) and third candidate for prime minister Suriya Jungrungruangkit (L), pose for a photo after drawing the electoral number nine, which will be used for the general election campaign during the prime minister candidates registration for the 2026 general election in Bangkok, Thailand, 28 December 2025. Thailand begins nationwide registration of constituency candidates to kick off the general election scheduled for 08 February 2026 after Prime Minister Anutin Charnvirakul dissolved the House of Representatives.
