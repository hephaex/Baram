# &#x27;Avatar 3&#x27; becomes fastest 2025 film to top 5 mln admissions in S. Korea

**연합뉴스** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

Avatar 3-box office&#x27;Avatar 3&#x27; becomes fastest 2025 film to top 5 mln admissions in S. Korea SEOUL, Jan. 2 (Yonhap) -- Disney&#x27;s &quot;Avatar: Fire And Ash&quot; has become the fastest 2025 release to surpass 5 million admissions in South Korea, box-office data showed Friday. The sci-fi tentpole drew about 358,000 moviegoers Thursday, taking a 50.7 percent revenue share and maintaining its lead at the local box office, according to the Korean Film Council. The movie&#x27;s cumulative admissions stood at about 5.01 million as of 7 a.m.

The third entry in James Cameron&#x27;s 16-year-old blockbuster franchise reached the 5 million mark in 17 days after its release, two days faster than Disney&#x27;s popular animated sequel &quot;Zootopia 2.&quot; &quot;Zootopia 2&quot; placed second Thursday with around 137,000 viewers and a 16.2 percent revenue share. It has attracted a total of 7.84 million moviegoers since its local release on Nov. 26.
