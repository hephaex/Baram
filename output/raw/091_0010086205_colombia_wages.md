# COLOMBIA WAGES

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Colombia raises minimum wage by 23.7 percent for 2026Colombian President Gustavo Petro (L) attends a military promotion ceremony alongside Colombian Defense Minister, retired General Pedro Sanchez, in Bogota, Colombia, 29 December 2025. The Colombian government announced a 23.7 percent increase in the minimum wage, set at two million pesos (about 535 US dollars) for 2026, including the transportation subsidy, according to Petro.
