# THAILAND ELECTIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Prime minister candidates&#x27; registration for 2026 general election in ThailandThaikaomai Party&#x27;s leader and prime ministerial candidate Suchatvee Suwansawat (2-L) draws the number 49 for his party&#x27;s election campaign during the prime minister candidates registration for the 2026 general election in Bangkok, Thailand, 28 December 2025.
