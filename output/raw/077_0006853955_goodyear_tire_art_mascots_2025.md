# Goodyear Tire Art Mascots 2025

**AP연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Goodyear Tire Art Mascots 2025IMAGE DISTRIBUTED FOR GOODYEAR - Goodyear honors the competitors of the 90th Goodyear Cotton Bowl Classic, Ohio State and Miami, with intricate, handcrafted tire sculptures of their team mascots made by artist Blake McFarland. First revealed on Monday, Dec. 29 after more than 560 hours of work, the sculptures will be donated to the respective schools after the game on Wednesday, Dec. 31 at AT&amp;T Stadium in Arlington, Texas. Image taken on Sunday, Dec. 28, 2025 in Arlington, Texas. (Brandon Wade/AP Content Services for Goodyear) THIS PHOTO WAS TAKEN BY AP CONTENT SERVICES FOR GOODYEAR
