# [News Today] Pres. Lee delivers New Year’s address

**KBS** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]President Lee Jae Myung laid out his roadmap for the second year of his administration.In his New Year&#x27;s address, he called for a shift away from capital-centered growth toward development led by the regions.He also stressed a new growth model grounded in safety and driven by culture, while underscoring peace as a foundation for stability.[REPORT]President Lee Jae Myung&#x27;s first public schedule in the New Year was visiting the Seoul National Cemetery with his advisors and cabinet members.He placed flowers and burned incense as he offered a silent prayer for patriots.On the guest book, the president wrote, &quot;I will open a world where people live together and the first year of Korea&#x27;s great leap forward with the people of Korea.&quot;In his New Year&#x27;s address, President Lee presented a roadmap for his second year in office.Lee Jae Myung / PresidentThe goals of the people&#x27;s sovereign government are clear for the New Year.We will achieve extensive leaps forward and growth in all areas.He said the growth strategy of selecting and concentrating on a few areas has shown its limitations and presented five transformation principles to change the nation&#x27;s growth paradigm.Those principles include transitioning from metropolitan-centered growth to growth led by regions outside the capital and from conglomerate-centered growth to growth enjoyed by all.Lee Jae Myung / PresidentWe will eradicate expediencies and unfairness remaining in various areas of society and build a society free of cheating and special privileges.He also unveiled visions for sustainable growth grounded in safety, growth led by culture, and stable growth backed by peace.Lee Jae Myung / PresidentThis year, I will again actively support U.S.–North Korea dialogue as a pacemaker and seek ways to restore inter-Korean relations.The words that appeared most frequently in his New Year&#x27;s address were &quot;growth&quot;
