# MEXICO ACCIDENT

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Attorney General&#x27;s Office investigates causes of train accident that left 13 dead in Southern MexicoRescue teams secure the area where the Trans-Isthmus train derailed in the municipality of Chivela, Oaxaca, Mexico, 29 December 2025. The Attorney General&#x27;s Office (FGR) of Mexico reported that it is conducting several investigations to clarify the cause of the derailment of a train on the Interoceanic Corridor of the Isthmus of Tehuantepec, which left 13 dead and forced the temporary suspension of train service in the area.
