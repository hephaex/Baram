# A virtual gallery

**코리아중앙데일리** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

LG Electronics announced on Dec. 30 that it is unveiling its new &quot;LG Gallery TV&quot; at CES 2026 in Las Vegas, on Jan. 6, 2026. The LG Gallery TV is LG’s new lifestyle display, designed both as a premium television and an art-inspired digital canvas. It integrates with the LG Gallery+ service, offering access to thousands of curated artworks, game illustrations and even AI-generated visuals. [LG Electronics]
