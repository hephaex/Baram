# SPAIN WEATHER

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

At least two dead after torrential rains in southern SpainCivil Guards carry out a search operation for a missing man in Alhaurin El Grande, Malaga, Spain, 29 December 2025. The missing man was swept away by the current as he and another man tried to cross Fahala river in a van, the previous day. The body of one of them has already been found. Another man died in Illora, Granada, also southern Spain, after his motorbike was also swept by the current due to the torrential rains on 28 December. EPA/Carlos Diaz
