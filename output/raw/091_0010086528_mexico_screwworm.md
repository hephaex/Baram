# MEXICO SCREWWORM

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-31 00:00 | 

---

Producers in southern Mexico introduce homemade traps to combat screwwormA homemade trap designed to control the screwworm fly is seen in Tapachula, Mexico, 30 December 2025. The Beef Cattle Production System Committee in Chiapas, working with the Ministry of Agriculture and Rural Development, presented the handcrafted device as part of efforts to curb the pest along the border with Guatemala.
