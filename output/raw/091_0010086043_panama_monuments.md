# PANAMA MONUMENTS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-30 00:00 | 

---

Panama to relocate Chinese community monuments back to original locationPolice tape surrounds the area where the Panamanian Chinese Monument was located in Panama City, Panama, 28 December 2025. The Panamanian government ordered the monument to the Chinese community to be restored to its original location at the Bridge of the Americas viewpoint after rejecting a demolition move by the Municipality of Arraijan. EPA/GABRIEL RODRIGUEZ
