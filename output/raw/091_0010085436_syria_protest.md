# SYRIA PROTEST

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

Syrian Alawites protest in Latakia after mosque bombingPeople carry an injured man at Soued Hospital after clashes erupted during a protest by members of the Alawite community in the city of Latakia, Syria, 28 December 2025. The protesters gathered against attacks targeting the Alawite community after a bombing at the Imam Ali bin Abi Talib Mosque in an Alawite neighborhood of Homs province on 26 December, in which at least eight people were killed and more than a dozen injured.
