---

Floods affect several towns in southern SpainFurniture and toys damaged by floods are left on a street during cleanup operations the morning after floods affected several parts of the town of Estacion de Cartama following the overflow of the Guadalhorce River, in Malaga, southern Spain, 28 December 2025. Heavy rains caused flooding in several towns in the province of Malaga last night, leading to evacuations, rescues, and property damage. EPA/CARLOS DIAZ
