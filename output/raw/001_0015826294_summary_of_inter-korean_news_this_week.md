NK weekly-inter-Korean newsSummary of inter-Korean news this week SEOUL, Jan. 2 (Yonhap) -- The following is a summary of inter-Korean news this week.------------N. Korea&#x27;s Kim logs most public appearances in nine years in 2025 SEOUL -- North Korean leader Kim Jong-un made the most public appearances in nine years in 2025, as he increased diplomatic engagement and on-site supervision of the implementation of a five-year national development plan, data showed Sunday.
