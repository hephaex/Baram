# MYANMAR ELECTIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Myanmar general electionMyanmar voters arrive to cast ballots during the first phase of general election at a polling station in Naypyitaw, the capital city of Myanmar, 28 December 2025. Myanmar&#x27;s military government holds its first national vote since the February 2021 coup in a multi-stage general election, with the first phase set for 28 December 2025, and the second for 11 January 2026. Over 50 political parties have registered to participate in the election, according to the Union Election Commission (UEC), while the main pro-democracy opposition, the National League for Democracy (NLD), has been dissolved since March 2023. EPA/RUNGROJ YONGRIT
