# [News Today] Lee Hye-hoon nominated as budget minister

**KBS** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]President Lee Jae Myung nominated former three-term conservative lawmaker Lee Hye-hoon to lead the new Ministry of Planning and Budget.Cheong Wa Dae says the pick reflects the president&#x27;s pledge to put pragmatism over ideology in economic policy.The People Power Party responded by immediately expelling her.[REPORT]Former conservative lawmaker Lee Hye-hoon has been nominated to head the Ministry of Planning and Budget, which is to be separated from the Ministry of Economy and Finance next month.Lee is a three-term lawmaker from parties affiliated with the People Power Party.She is also an economic expert who served as floor manager of the National Assembly&#x27;s Special Committee on Budget and Accounts.Shortly after her nomination, Lee issued a statement saying that economic and livelihood issues can be solved through cooperation beyond political views and ideologies.She pledged to do her best to carry out the Lee Jae Myung administration&#x27;s policy goals of achieving both welfare and growth, and pursuing sustainable growth.Former lawmaker Kim Song-sik from the former Grand National Party and People&#x27;s Party has been nominated as vice chair of the National Economic Advisory Council, a minister-level post.Cheong Wa Dae said the unconventional nominations of opposition figures reflect the president&#x27;s principle of unity and pragmatism.Lee Kyu-yeon / Pres. secretary for public relations and communicationThe nominations are in line with the personnel appointment principle.These figures are highly recognized in the areas of the economy and budgeting.The People Power Party immediately convened an emergency supreme council meeting to expel Lee Hye-hoon from the party.The PPP said Lee has committed the worst act against her party as a sitting regional party chair by voluntarily choosing to collaborate with the current administration with the local elections just six months away.The ruling Democratic Party pushed back by saying the
