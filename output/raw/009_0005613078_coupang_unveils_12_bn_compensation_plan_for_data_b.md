# Coupang unveils $1.2 bn compensation plan for data breach incident

**매일경제** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

South Korean e-commerce giant Coupang Inc. announced on Monday that it will implement a customer compensation package worth 1.68 trillion won ($1.17 billion) in connection with a recent personal data breach incident.In a press release, Coupang said it will begin issuing purchase vouchers starting January 15.The compensation will cover customers tied to 33.7 million accounts that received data breach notifications in late November.Both subscription and non-subscription customers are eligible, as are former users who closed their accounts after receiving the notification.Coupang said it will notify eligible customers by text message on a rolling basis with instructions on how to use the vouchers.Each eligible customer will receive four single use vouchers with a combined value of 50,000 won. The vouchers can be used for general shopping on the platform, including fast delivery, overseas purchases, and marketplace items, as well as food delivery, travel related services, and premium product offerings.Customers will be able to view their vouchers in the Coupang app starting January 15 and apply them at checkout. The company said additional details will be provided separately.Harold Rogers, Coupang’s chief administrative officer and acting chief executive officer, said the compensation reflects the company’s responsibility to customers following the incident.He added that Coupang aims to fully address the issue and rebuild trust with its users.
