# PERU NEW YEAR TRADITIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-31 00:00 | 

---

Peruvians prepare to burn effigies of public figures on New Year&#x27;s EveNew Year&#x27;s Eve effigies with the faces of former Lima mayor and Peruvian presidential candidate Rafael Lopez Aliaga and interim president Jose Jeri are sold in Lima, Peru, 30 December 2025. The effigies, traditionally burned at year&#x27;s end to symbolize letting go of the past, include former president Dina Boluarte and popular TikToker Jusseira Mayumi, known as &#x27;Moca&#x27;.
