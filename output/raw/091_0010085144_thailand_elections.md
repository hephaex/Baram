# THAILAND ELECTIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Prime minister candidates&#x27; registration for 2026 general election in ThailandPheu Thai Party&#x27;s prime ministerial candidate Yotsanun Wongsawat (C), son of former Prime Minister Somchai Wongsawat and Yaowapa, sister of Thaksin Shinawatra, poses for a photo after his party drew the number nine for its election campaign during the prime minister candidates registration for the 2026 general election in Bangkok, Thailand, 28 December 2025. Thailand begins nationwide registration of constituency candidates to kick off the general election scheduled for 08 February 2026 after Prime Minister Anutin Charnvirakul dissolved the House of Representatives.
