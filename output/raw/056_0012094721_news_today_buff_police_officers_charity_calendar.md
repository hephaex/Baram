# [News Today] Buff police officers’ charity calendar

**KBS** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

[LEAD]Every year around this time, a group of police officers step in front of the camera, not for publicity, but for a cause.Known as the &quot;muscle-fit police,&quot; they have spent the past eight years raising funds to support children who are victims of crime.Here&#x27;s our last story.[REPORT]These people flexing and posing for the camera,are real officers featured in the 2026 police calendars.They are all police officers in active service.The proceeds from the calendar sale will go to victims of child abuse.Lieutenant Kwon Hyeon-ju, stationed in Pohang, Gyeongsangbuk-do Province, also posed for the camera.Her parents lost their home in a devastating wildfire that swept across the Gyeongbuk area back in March.Having received help and donations from people from all walks of life, she wanted to spread the kindness she had received back then.Kwon Hyeon-ju / Pohang Bukbu Police StationThe house where they had lived for 50 years was completely burned down.The donations people collected back then were a big help to us.Lieutenant Kim Tae-gyeong was injured several times while chasing down criminals.Kim Tae-gyeong / Pyeongtaek Police StationI fell from a high structure as I was chasing an offender and injured my leg.I had four surgeries.He started working out to strengthen his body and mind.Now he is healthy enough to participate in a fitness contest.Kim Tae-gyeong / Pyeongtaek Police StationI was eager to take part after hearing that I could help abused children.The police calendar sale began eight years ago to help victims of child abuse.The donation exceeded one hundred million won, or nearly 70 thousand U.S. dollars, as of last year.■ 제보하기▷ 전화 : 02-781-1234, 4444▷ 이메일 : kbs1234@kbs.co.kr▷ 카카오톡 : &#x27;KBS제보&#x27; 검색, 채널 추가▷ 네이버, 유튜브에서 KBS뉴스를 구독해주세요!
