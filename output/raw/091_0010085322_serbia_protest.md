# SERBIA PROTEST

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Students in Belgrade collect signatures demanding early parliamentary electionsStudents collect signatures for the &#x27;Call for Victory&#x27; campaign in Belgrade, Serbia, 28 December 2025. Students across Serbia are organizing nationwide protests to collect signatures demanding early parliamentary elections. The demonstrations are part of a wave of civil unrest that began following the fatal canopy collapse at the Novi Sad railway station in late 2024, with students now calling for accountability and social justice.
