# SPAIN TRADITIONS

**EPA연합뉴스** | 2025-12-28 00:00 | 

---

Traditional dance to mark Spanish Fools&#x27; Day in CordobaPeople wearing traditional outfits take part in the Dance of the Fools to mark Spanish Fools&#x27;s Day in the town of Fuente Carreteros, in Cordoba, Andalusia, southern Spain, 28 December 2025. The Dance of Fools and Dance of the Bear, dated back to 1765, are the two only dances brought by European colonizers that settled down in Andalusia and are still performed nowadays. EPA/Salas
