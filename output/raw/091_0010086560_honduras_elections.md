Current mayor of Tegucigalpa refuses to recognize election resultsThe mayor of Tegucigalpa, Jorge Aldana, speaks during a press conference in Tegucigalpa, Honduras, 30 December 2025. Aldana said he will not recognize the results of the 30 November general elections nor relinquish the mayoralty until 435 tally sheets that he claims prove his victory are counted.
