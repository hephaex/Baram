# T-50 trainer jet makes emergency landing; no casualties reported: Air Force

**연합뉴스** | 2026-01-02 00:00 | 

---

Air Force-incident T-50 trainer jet makes emergency landing; no casualties reported: Air Force SEOUL, Jan. 2 (Yonhap) -- A T-50 training aircraft made an emergency landing during flight training on Friday, but no casualties were reported, the Air Force said. Two pilots were aboard the aircraft when it made the emergency landing at around 2:36 p.m. after an engine warning lamp turned on, according to the armed service. The aircraft remains turned over after derailing from the runway during landing, but all crew members are in a safe condition, it added. The Air Force has established an investigative team to determine the exact cause of the accident.
