# Current Woori Financial chief again tapped for 3-year chairmanship

**연합뉴스** | 2025-12-29 00:00 | 

---

Woori Financial-chairmanship Current Woori Financial chief again tapped for 3-year chairmanship SEOUL, Dec. 29 (Yonhap) -- Yim Jong-yong, the current chairman of Woori Financial Group Co., was picked Monday to lead the South Korean major financial group for another three years, group officials said. Yim was selected as the sole final candidate for the position by the group&#x27;s executive candidate recommendation committee following an interview process conducted earlier in the day. Final approval will be made at the group&#x27;s shareholders&#x27; meeting in March next year. He was first appointed chairman in March 2023. &quot;During his tenure, he succeeded in entering the securities business and acquiring an insurance company, completing a comprehensive financial group portfolio. He also played a key role in strengthening financial stability,&quot; a company official said. Woori Financial Group is one of the country&#x27;s leading financial holding companies, providing services in commercial banking and credit cards.
